Long-term career goal and pathway:

I don’t have a specific goal in mind in terms of a career, but one thing I do know is that
I want to set up my own shop sometime in the future.

Human skill I would like to develop at EDA:

People often describe me as “chill” and “laid-back” and I accept that description and in
a way, I do like it. But I want to be able to captivate an audience, give off good first
impressions.

Description of my strength and limitation:

My biggest strengths are willpower, determination, and focus. The limitation however,
is once I get decent at something, I get bored and because of that I have never
become truly good at something. I want to change that and I think I can do that at EDA.

How I will manage workload:

Something vital that I have failed to do time and time again is talk to the
people responsible for the course or work on how to manage the work effectively.
I have the determination and willpower to push through the work, but I will manage
myself and work productively with all stakeholders by first learning how.

How and where I will seek help in a timely manner:

During foundations, my go to for help will be facilitators. I can speak to my facilitator
Joseph but since he is working remote, if at any point in time I feel like I need
face-to-face interaction for assistance I can always ask the facilitators on campus or
talk to my seniors.

My expectation from the facilitation team:

Best advice possible.

Scheduling block-out times:

None I can think of.
